Sia Amun — Artist, Songwriter & Mentor
The Story

BORN
INTO It

The daughter of TROOP's lead singer and 4x Grammy-winning songwriter-producer Steven Russell Harts, Sia Amun grew up with heavy expectation — and chose merit over legacy.

Her journey hasn't been glamorous. After a turbulent youth that included eviction at 15, Sia had to choose between the streets and the path of her own becoming. Music became the place she survived, healed, and rebuilt. She found her footing in writers' rooms and late-night studio sessions, shaping her pen and voice while working behind the scenes with icons like Mary J. Blige, Trey Songz, and Lady Gaga.

The deepest shift in Sia's evolution came through grief. Losing her mother and navigating heartbreak forced her into a different kind of growth. That pain became the soil where her new music bloomed. Today, her art reflects a woman who has been cracked open but chose transformation over bitterness.

Sia Amun is sensual, spiritual, and rooted in truth. As a 4x Platinum certified songwriter, her velvet tone is wrapped around emotional honesty and nostalgic soul that gives listeners the space to feel, reflect, and heal.
