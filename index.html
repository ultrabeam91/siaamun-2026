Her Story

BORN
INTO It

Born into music royalty as the daughter of TROOP's lead singer and 4x Grammy-winning songwriter-producer Steven Russell Harts, SIA AMUN grew up with heavy expectation. But rather than ride the coattails of legacy, SIA chose to earn her name on merit, learning early that the industry doesn't hand you a career — you have to build it yourself.

Her journey hasn't been glamorous. After a turbulent youth that included eviction at 15, SIA had to choose between the streets and the path of her own becoming. Music became the place she survived, healed, and rebuilt. She found her footing in writers' rooms and late-night studio sessions, shaping her pen and her voice while working behind the scenes with icons like Mary J. Blige, Trey Songz, and Lady Gaga, to name a few. Those years didn't just sharpen her craft — they taught her resilience.

But the deepest shift in SIA's evolution came through grief. Losing her Mother and people she loved, navigating heartbreak, and facing herself in the quiet forced her into a different kind of growth. That pain became the soil where her new music bloomed. Today, her art reflects a woman who has been cracked open but chose transformation over bitterness.

SIA AMUN is sensual, spiritual, and rooted in truth. As a 4x Platinum certified songwriter, her voice carries the weight of lived experience but flows with the softness of self-awareness. Her velvet tone is wrapped around emotional honesty and her nostalgic soul sound gives listeners the space to feel, reflect, and heal. She creates love songs that pour into men, uplift women, and honor the balance of both.

At her core, SIA is on a mission bigger than music: to embody true love, to represent God in human form through her authenticity, and to inspire people to live in their truth. Every song is a prayer, every lyric a mirror, every performance a reminder that healing is possible.

She has graced stages alongside Ziggy Marley, Keyshia Cole, Eric Bellinger, YG, Kenyon Dixon, D Smoke, SIR, AJ McQueen and more, yet she is only just stepping into her fullest form. With new music shaped by evolution, faith, and self-discovery, SIA AMUN stands as proof that legacy may open doors, but purpose, truth, and divine alignment are what keep them open.
